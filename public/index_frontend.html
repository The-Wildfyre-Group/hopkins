This is our moment

A first-of-its kind call for Black men around the nation to come together to make a bold statement that we are taking ownership of our health and collective destiny.

For far too long, the collective health of Black men in America has paled significantly when compared to outcomes experienced by other male communities throughout the country. Simply put, the state of health of the Black male is one of the greatest crisis facing Black men in America.

THE TEAM BEHIND OUR CHALLENGE

The Black Men’s Health Challenge is driven by the larger, most comprehensive study of Black men’s health in American history. This historic effort to better understand the health challenges facing our men is unprecedented and has the potential to empower generations young and old.


For many years, the Black men have been a virtually “invisible” audience with the larger national and international medical communities. Little research into the unique health needs and hurdles of Black men has been done, leading to significant education gaps and dramatically poor outcomes.


The Black Men’s Health Challenge is our chance to take a major step towards changing this trend.

The Black Men’s Health Challenge is led by Black men who are among the leading medical researchers in America, representing some of the world’s top universities. Universities contributing to the Black Men’s Health Challenge include:


  • John’s Hopkins University

  • George Washington University

  • Other schools to be included
